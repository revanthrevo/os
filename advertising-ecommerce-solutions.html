Email Marketing

Email marketing is a process of sending targeted email messages to potential customers or already paying customers in order to promote a product offer. Email marketing can be effective in reaching a large number of people quickly and inexpensively. Building email lists takes time though, so it’s important to start building them as soon as possible.

Content Marketing

Content marketing is the creation and distribution of valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience. The goal is to create content that people will want to share, which will then help to increase your brand awareness and drive relevant traffic to your website.

User-Generated Content

User-generated content is any content created by customers or fans of a product or service. This can include blog posts, reviews, comments, images, videos. UGC can be a valuable tool for your E-Commerce business, as it helps to build trust and loyalty with customers. In addition you can use this content in paid marketing(as advertisements).

SMS Marketing

SMS marketing is the process of sending text messages to customers or potential customers in order to promote a product or service. SMS marketing can be very effective in reaching a large number of people quickly and relatively inexpensively. SMS marketing is still quite underrated and has a lot of growth potential in 2022 and beyond.
